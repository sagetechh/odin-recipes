Cucumber Salad

Refreshing Cucumber Salad

Cool off with this refreshing cucumber salad recipe

  • 4 cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1 small white onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon dried dill, or to taste


  1. Toss sliced cucumbers and onion together in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Combine vinegar, sugar, and water in a saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil;
    pour over cucumbers and onions in the bowl.
  3. Stir in dill. Cover and let marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
